✅ Who falls in this category?

  • New to the gym (0–6 months of consistent training).
  • Learning proper form, basic exercises, and workout structure.
  • Can lift light weights but lacks significant muscle strength or endurance.
  • Struggles with mind-muscle connection.

Details

✅ Signs of a beginner:

  • Bench press below 0.75x body weight.
  • Squat below body weight.
  • Struggles to perform more than 10 push-ups or bodyweight squats.
  • Gets fatigued quickly in workouts.

✅ Training Focus:

  • Learning proper form.
  • Building consistency.
  • Strengthening stabilizer muscles and tendons.
  • Developing endurance and work capacity.

Guidelines

Essential steps for beginners:

  1. Set Clear Goals: Decide whether you want to gain muscle, lose fat, increase endurance, or improve overall fitness.
  2. Learn Basic Exercises: Strength training: squats, push-ups, lunges, deadlifts, bench press, rows. Cardio: treadmill, cycling, jump rope.
  3. Focus on Proper Form: Learn correct posture and technique to prevent injuries.
  4. Follow a Balanced Routine: Warm-up 5–10 mins, workout 3–5 times/week, cool-down stretches.
  5. Start Full-Body Workouts: Train major muscle groups 3–4 times a week before splitting into body parts.
  6. Manage Rest & Recovery: Take rest days and get 7–9 hours of sleep.
  7. Maintain a Healthy Diet: Eat protein-rich foods, complex carbs, and stay hydrated.
  8. Track Progress: Keep a workout log to monitor improvements.
  9. Avoid Overtraining: Gradually increase intensity.
  10. Stay Consistent & Patient: Consistency is key for results.
