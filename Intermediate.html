Details.

✅ Signs of an intermediate:

  • Bench press 0.75x to 1.25x body weight.
  • Squat around body weight or slightly more.
  • Can perform 15+ push-ups or bodyweight squats easily.
  • Visible muscle definition and better endurance.
  • Recovery optimized with proper nutrition and sleep.

✅ Training Focus:

  • Progressive overload.
  • Workout splits (upper/lower, push/pull/legs).
  • Increase strength and muscle mass.
  • Maintain proper form and prevent injuries.
